Welcome to Coverage Maximization Game

Text Instructions:

1. Players in this game are represented by tokens
2. You control the token with a lock on it

3. Each player's agent cover tiles in the surrounding (indicated by green tiles), except obstacles (black tiles)

4. If multiple agents are near each other, an overlap occurs as indicated by dark green tiles

5. You and other players can only see tiles within 3 ranges away

6. You can move your agent to any of the neighboring tiles (or stay in position), valid moves will be highlighted in yellow when you hover over them

7. This is a cooperative game. You and your teammates work together to cover as many coins as possible.
8. There are 4 rounds in this game. At the end of each round, you will be given a quick survey.
9. Within a round, players are randomly chosen to move. When it is your turn, the texts 'Your Move' will be displayed.
